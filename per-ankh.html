Ethos & Organizational Structure

Organizational framework and spiritual principles of the Per-Ankh Temple and Entheogenic Church

Our Sacred Mission

Per Ankh Entheogenic Church is a religious organization dedicated to the spiritual upliftment of our people and all people throughout the world and the betterment of life on planet earth.

Founding Members

KILINDI IYI is the founding member of the Per-Ankh temple and Entheogenic Church alongside BABA MOUDOU BAQUI and AYANA IYI. They bring their collective wisdom, knowledge, talents, information and insights to rebuild, restore and resurrect ancient teachings of spiritual cultivation that enhances the truest Identity and awakens the God presence within every neophyte.

Seat of Elders

The seat of elders is the place of honor for 12 community elders seasoned in significant aspects of the development of the self, family and community. The seat of the elders is comprised of 6 men and 6 women who will gently guide and advice the founding members in the spirit of wisdom and Love.

Study Group

The study group is designed to provide the neophyte with structure and the basic tools by which to build an appreciation for and knowledge "the self". Further, the study group is designed to encourage the seeking and fulfillment of each neophyte's life purpose. The core knowledge base of the neophyte is founded on the principles of Ma'at (Justice, Order, Reciprocity, Harmony, Balance, Truth and Propriety) and includes but is not limited to:

  • Organizational skills through (Roberts Rules of Order)
  • Truth (History and Culture of Black people from the continent America and the Diaspora)
  • Justice (Knowledge of the Law, public and private)
  • Propriety (Proper Conduct, Etiquette, Protocol)
  • Reciprocity (Give and Take, Exchange of Energy)
  • Balance (Equilibrium, Harmony, Moderation)
  • Order (Structure, Organization, Sequence)
  • Harmony (Unity, Agreement, Concord)

Initiation Process

The process of initiation is the systematic process by which the neophyte is transformed from a state of limited awareness to one of expanded consciousness. The process of initiation is designed to awaken the neophyte to his/her highest potential and to prepare him/her for service to humanity and the planet. The process of initiation involves the study of Alchemy/High Science to access or awaken the inner light that will allow him/her to unify with the cosmic forces as well as the harnessing cosmic energy to manifest the desired reality.

Upon completion of the initiation process the Djedhi will re-enter the community with vigor and purpose.

Healing Center

Health and wellbeing, affecting our physical, mental, emotional and spiritual aspects, (Holistic Approach) The Healing center is the purification process where initiates learn to heal themselves with the Self-Help Technology for emotional Health as well as herbal healing modalities. The expectation for each initiate is "Man/Woman Know and heal Thyself". The commitment and intention for self-healing is paramount for each initiate to progress through the levels of initiation. Without this vital process, the initiate remains burdened by the emotional "Physical Scars" that weigh on the heart.
