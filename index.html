WHO ARE WE?

We are a community of visionaries exploring the cutting-edge of awakening through psychedelics and sacred plant medicines.

As worldwide interest in psychedelics expands, we are finding ourselves in the midst of a Psychedelic Renaissance and Global Shamanic Revival, an exciting intersection of groundbreaking science, cultural exchange, cutting-edge therapy, and game-changing personal development.

At Per Ankh we provide deeply researched content about psychedelics – primarily natural psychedelics long-used by indigenous people around the world – and how you can work with them for healing and spiritual growth. We explore these topics through the multifaceted framework of science, culture, and spirituality.
