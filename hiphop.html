SESSION FORMATS

Cipher Ceremonies

Group healing sessions where participants form a circle, pass the mic, and share freestyle expressions while supported by live beats and plant medicine guidance.

Duration: 2-4 hours
Participants: 6-12 people
Focus: Community healing, collective expression

Studio Sanctuaries

Recording sessions transformed into spiritual practice, where the booth becomes a confessional and the track becomes a healing testimony.

Duration: 4-8 hours
Participants: 1-4 artists + producer
Focus: Individual healing, artistic therapy

Freestyle Flow States

Improvisation sessions where participants tap into stream of consciousness, allowing subconscious wisdom to emerge through spontaneous lyrical expression.

Duration: 1-3 hours
Participants: 3-8 people
Focus: Consciousness expansion, creative breakthrough

Beat Meditation

Mindfulness practice using instrumental hip-hop tracks as meditation anchors, focusing breath and consciousness on rhythmic patterns and basslines.

Duration: 30-90 minutes
Participants: 5-20 people
Focus: Mindfulness, stress relief, inner peace

Lyric Therapy

Structured writing sessions where participants process trauma, set intentions, and explore emotions through bar construction and wordplay as therapeutic tools.

Duration: 2-4 hours
Participants: 4-10 people
Focus: Emotional processing, trauma healing

All-Format Integration

Comprehensive healing retreats combining all session types over multiple days, creating complete transformation experiences.

Duration: 3-7 days
Participants: 8-15 people
Focus: Complete spiritual overhaul
