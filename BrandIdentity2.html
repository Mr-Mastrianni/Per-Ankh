Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Per-Ankh Temple and Entheogenic Church is a private humanitarian and charitable spiritual society, committed to furthering the development, uplifting, enlightenment and spiritual realization through education and spiritual practices, left to us by the ancient teachings of our forefathers.

We are concerned with the general welfare and spiritual enlightenment of all indigenous people and all people throughout the world and the betterment of life on planet earth.

Our Vision

"The true Pioneers in the field of psychedelics have still yet to emerge from the shadows to share their own personal experiences"

We envision a world where the ancient wisdom of entheogenic plants is honored, researched, and safely accessible for spiritual growth, healing, and the expansion of human consciousness in the midst of our Psychedelic Renaissance and Global Shamanic Revival.
