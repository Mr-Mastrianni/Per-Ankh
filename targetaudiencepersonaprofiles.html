Common Objections & Our Responses

Objection: "Psychedelics are dangerous drugs"

Response: We emphasize sacred plant medicines used in religious context with proper education, safety protocols, and community support - not recreational drug use.

Objection: "This isn't real religion"

Response: Per Ankh is legally recognized as interfaith religious organization with deep roots in ancient Egyptian spiritual traditions and contemporary entheogenic practices.

Objection: "Legal concerns and risks"

Response: We operate within religious freedom protections, provide legal guidance, and advocate for entheogenic rights while prioritizing member safety and compliance.

Objection: "Too intense or overwhelming"

Response: We offer graduated education, micro-dosing programs, study groups, and comprehensive preparation - no one is pressured into experiences beyond their comfort level.
